How It Works
Safe housing in hours,
not years.
Our process is fast, discreet, and designed to protect you at every step.
📞
01
Call or text our hotline
Reach us any time. You can text if you can't speak. Our team speaks multiple languages and will never share your identity.
🔍
02
Caseworker assessment
A trained caseworker understands your needs — children, pets, location, language — and finds the right match.
🏡
03
Matched to a verified host
All hosts are police-checked, home-verified, and DV-awareness trained. You only see a first name and suburb.
🚗
04
Transport arranged
We coordinate safe transport to your host. You can be in a safe home within hours of calling.
📈
05
Stability & next steps
While you stay, we connect you to upskilling, legal advice, and long-term housing options through our partners.

Your Safety Comes First
Designed to keep
you invisible.
We built Hopely knowing that your location and identity could put you at risk. Every part of the system protects you.
🕵️
No real names in the system
You're identified only by a case code. Hosts never see your surname, photo, or contact details.
📵
Silent contact options
Can't speak aloud? Text us or use our discreet online form. We'll never call back without permission.
🔒
Location protected until placement
Host suburb is revealed only after you agree. Your current address is never recorded.
⚡
Quick Exit on every page
Hit the red "Quick Exit" button anytime — it instantly takes you to Google and clears this visit from your browser history.
